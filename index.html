about us

What is a "Jack Brew"?

Jack Brew Coffee is a dedication to the men and women in history that needed a break between battles. The moment between artillery barrages, the moments before a wagon of wounded soldiers need medical attention.

Those precious few minutes or hours when the bitterness of coffee was welcome as a distraction.

Inspired by the term itself – “Jack Brew” – this type of coffee was brewed by soldiers and served in a tin can, often consumed in the makeshift trenches and fox holes of a battlefield.
